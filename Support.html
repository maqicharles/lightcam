About the App

LightCam X is a 5-in-1 light utility toolkit combining flashlight, camera, strobe, screen light, and emergency beacon features — all in one intuitive app.

Features

  • 📸 HD photo & video capture with real-time preview
  • 🔦 Adjustable flashlight with strobe & brightness control
  • 🌈 Full-screen color lighting for reading, fill light, or ambiance
  • 🚨 Emergency strobe modes with built-in sounds (SOS, police, etc.)
  • 🌗 Screen glow fill light for soft lighting scenarios

FAQ

Q: The flashlight doesn’t work?
A: Please check camera/flashlight permissions in iOS Settings → LightCam X.

Q: How to switch between modes?
A: Tap the icons at the bottom of the home screen to toggle between the 5 tools.
