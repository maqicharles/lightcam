LightCam Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [May 15, 2025]

Welcome to use LightCam! We highly value your privacy. This privacy policy aims to explain the relevant rules and practices of the LightCam application (hereinafter referred to as "this application") when collecting, using, storing, and protecting your personal information. Please read and understand the entire content of this privacy policy carefully before using this application. By using this application, you indicate that you have fully understood and agreed to all the terms of this privacy policy.

I. Information We Collect

(A) Necessary Information

  • Camera permission-related information: To achieve professional-level camera functions, including the use of front and rear cameras, automatic/manual exposure adjustment, filter beautification, and high-definition video recording, we need to obtain your camera usage permission. During your use of the camera function, we only temporarily process the photo and video data you take and will not store or transmit this content without your consent, unless you actively choose to save it to the album or share it.
  • Flashlight and warning light permission-related information: When you use the flashlight (high beam, low beam, SOS mode, etc.) and warning light (red and blue warning flashing light) functions, we need to obtain the device's flash control permission. This permission is only used to control the device's hardware lights and will not collect any personal information.
  • Screen fill light and solid-color light permission-related information: To achieve the functions of freely selecting the color temperature and brightness of the screen fill light and the full-screen illumination and color-changing of the screen solid-color light, we need to obtain the screen display control permission. This permission is only used to adjust the screen display parameters and will not collect your personal information.

(B) Non-essential but Experience-Enhancing Information

  • Location information: If you choose to enable the location information permission, we can provide you with more scenario-appropriate lighting usage suggestions based on your location. For example, in an outdoor adventure scenario, we can recommend suitable warning light modes or fill light parameters for you. You can turn off this permission at any time in the device settings without affecting the use of other basic functions.
  • Album access permission: When you choose to save the photos or videos you take to the album or select materials from the album for editing, we need to obtain the album access permission. We only access the album content with your explicit authorization and will not read or transmit other data in the album without your consent.

II. Use of Information

  • The necessary information we collect is only used to implement the core functions of this application, such as camera shooting and light control.
  • For non-essential information, it is only used to optimize the application functions and enhance your user experience with your authorization and consent. For example, we can provide personalized recommendations based on location information and more suitable filters or editing suggestions based on album content.
  • We will not use your personal information for any illegal purposes, nor will we sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, unless we obtain your explicit consent or it is otherwise required by laws and regulations.

III. Storage of Information

  • We only retain your personal information for the shortest period necessary to implement the functions of this application. For example, the photo and video data you take temporarily but do not save will be deleted immediately after you exit the shooting interface.
  • We adopt reasonable technical and management measures to ensure the security of your personal information and prevent the information from being leaked, tampered with, or lost. We use encryption technology to protect the transmission and storage of sensitive information and regularly conduct security assessments and maintenance on the system.

IV. Third-Party Services

This application may integrate third-party services, such as data analysis tools, to help us understand the usage of the application and improve our services. When using third-party services, we will ensure that the third parties comply with strict privacy protection standards, obtain your information only within the necessary scope, and their use of information is subject to this privacy policy and the third parties' own privacy policies. We will try our best to supervise the third parties' handling of your personal information, but we cannot be responsible for the independent actions of the third parties.

V. Your Rights

  • Access and manage information: You can view and manage the various permissions granted to this application at any time through the device's privacy settings. For example, you can turn off the camera, album, location, and other permissions to restrict our access to the corresponding information.
  • Delete information: Under the premise of complying with laws and regulations, you have the right to request us to delete your personal information. You can submit a deletion request through the feedback channel in the application or by contacting our customer service (contact email: [catpadcharles@gmail.com]). We will verify your identity and process your request as soon as possible.
  • Withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent to the collection and use of certain information at any time. Withdrawing consent may affect your use of some functions but will not affect the information processing activities that have been carried out based on your previous consent.

VI. Children's Privacy Protection

We highly value the privacy protection of children. This application is not intended for children under 14 years old, and we will not knowingly collect the personal information of children under 14 years old. If we find that we have collected the personal information of children under 14 years old, we will immediately delete the relevant information. If you think we may have mistakenly collected children's personal information, please contact our customer service (contact email: [catpadcharles@gmail.com]).

VII. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may revise this privacy policy due to updates in laws and regulations, business development, or other reasons. The revised privacy policy will be published in this application and notified to you in a prominent way. If you continue to use this application, it means you accept the revised privacy policy. If you do not agree with the content of the changed agreement, you can stop using this application.
